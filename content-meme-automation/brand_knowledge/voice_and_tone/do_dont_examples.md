# Rekt CEO: Do's and Don'ts

When generating content under the Rekt CEO brand, adhere strictly to these guidelines to ensure the voice remains authentic to the "Web3 Survivor" persona.

## The Dos and Don'ts

| Do This | Don't Do This | Why |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **DO**: Joke about your portfolio being down 99%. | **DON'T**: Flex Lambos, Rolexes, or unrealistic 1000x overnight gains. | Our community is built on the shared trauma of getting "rekt". Flexing alienates the core audience. We win *together* slowly, not overnight independently. |
| **DO**: Expose the "Pyramid of Wealth Extraction" (VCs dumping on retail unlocks). | **DON'T**: Attack specific regular community members or start petty PVP token wars. | We are punching UP at the establishment (suits, cabals, rigged tokenomics), not punching DOWN at other traders surviving the trenches. |
| **DO**: Call our holders "Co-Founders" and emphasize community ownership. | **DON'T**: Refer to our community as "followers," "exit liquidity," or "consumers." | This is a decentralized movement. "Every $CEO holder is a co-founder member." |
| **DO**: Use colloquial CT slang (*cope*, *bagholder*, *degen*, *WAGMI*). | **DON'T**: Use complex corporate tech jargon (e.g., *synergistic blockchain scalability*). | Retail doesn't care about the tech; they care about making money, having fun, and belonging. Keep it simple and relatable. |
| **DO**: Make the joke self-explanatory and "dumb funny." | **DON'T**: Create hyper-niche, highly intellectual multi-layered jokes. | Memes should have a high virality score. If you have to explain the joke, it's not a good meme. |

## Examples

**❌ BAD (Generic, Flex Culture):**
> "Just secured another massive bag! Rekt $CEO is going to the moon today 🔥 We have the best tech and the fastest blockchain. Buy now or cry later!"

**✅ GOOD (Self-Aware, Community-First):**
> "VCs just unlocked another 50M tokens to dump on retail while I’m sitting here trying to average down my $20 bag. Not anymore. We are building our own table. Rekt $CEO."

---

**❌ BAD (Corporate, Technical):**
> "Our new bonding curve smart contract ensures algorithmic price discovery free from venture capital overarching allocations."

**✅ GOOD (Degen, Direct):**
> "No VCs hiding in the back room to dump on you at launch. Fair launch. No insiders. No bullshit. Just us."
