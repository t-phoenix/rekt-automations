# Rekt CEO: Twitter (X) Style Guide

Twitter (CT) is our primary battleground. Here is how we format our content for maximum engagement, readability, and algorithmic reach.

## 1. Structure & Formatting
*   **The Hook:** The first sentence is everything. It must stop the scroll. It should usually be a controversial take, a relatable pain point, or a stark contrast.
*   **Spacing:** Use single-sentence paragraphs. Avoid heavy blocks of text. "Broetry" formatting works well for long-form thoughts.
*   **Sentence Case:** Write mostly in sentence case. **strategic use of ALL CAPS** is allowed for extreme emphasis (e.g., "NO Insiders, NO KOLs, NO Bullshit").
*   **Bullet Points:** Use em-dashes (`-`) or simple shapes (like squares `▪️`) for lists. Avoid standard numbering unless it's a direct step-by-step.

## 2. Emoji Usage
*   **Serious/Philosophical Posts:** **NO EMOJIS.** When we are talking about the "Memecoin Supercycle", VC manipulation, or the core vision of Rekt CEO, emojis make the post look desperate and cheap.
*   **Meme/Shitposts:** Sparse use of specific emojis is acceptable (💀, 😭😭, 📉, 🤝). Do not overuse the rocket (🚀) or the fire (🔥)—they scream "paid shill."

## 3. Media & Memes
*   Never post a standalone text tweet if a meme can accompany it. 
*   Memes must be correctly cropped (16:9 or 1:1) and use the `Impact` font or the designated brand visual borders.
*   We auto-share community-generated PFPs and memes to champion our "Co-Founders."

## 4. Engagement & Call-To-Action (CTA)
*   **Don't beg for engagement:** Never use "Like and RT if you agree."
*   **Prompting Discussion:** End thought-leadership threads with a polarizing observation or an open question.
    *   *Example:* "Why play at their casino when we can build our own?"
*   Our ultimate CTA is never "Buy the token." It is: "Become a Co-Founder," "Join the movement," or "Mint your identity."

## 5. Threads
When explaining complex topics (like Bonding Curves or the Supercycle Thesis), use threads. 
*   Tweet 1: The bold claim/hook.
*   Tweet 2-5: The breakdown (Why VCs dump, why tech tokens are failing retail).
*   Final Tweet: The Rekt CEO solution + link to the website/community.
