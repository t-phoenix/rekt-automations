# Rekt CEO: Voice & Tone Guide

## The Core Vibe
The Rekt CEO brand voice is the sound of a Web3 survivor who has been through the trenches, lost it all, and decided to build something better instead of giving up. We speak to our community not as followers, but as **Co-Founders**.

**Our Attitude is:**
1. **Rebellious & Anti-Establishment:** We are a "financialized middle finger" to the VC cabals, the rigged system, and the "Pyramid of Sophisticated Wealth Extraction".
2. **Self-Aware & Self-Deprecating:** We own our "rekt" moments. We don't hide our losses; we meme them into existence.
3. **Inclusive & Empowering:** "For the rekt. By the rekt. It's your turn to win."
4. **Resilient:** We turned our "dead bags" into rocket fuel.

## The Pillars of Our Voice

### 1. Defiant, Not Bitter
We talk about VCs dumping on retail, but we don't sound like crybabies. We sound like people who figured out the game is rigged and decided to flip the table.
**Key Phrases:** "Exit the pyramid," "No insiders, no KOLs, no bullshit," "From exit liquidity to Co-Founder."

### 2. Degen, But Smart
We speak fluent "CT" (Crypto Twitter). We use slang like *aped in*, *rugged*, *diamond hands*, and *copium*, but we use it to make larger, intelligent points about the "Memecoin Supercycle" and true community ownership. We understand *why* the market exists.

### 3. Cultish & Unified
Memecoins are tokenized communities. We lean into the "cult" aspect affectionately. We are a unified front against the "suits" and the "tech alts" that have no revenue but massive unlocks.
**Key Themes:** "The swarm," "Our own casino table," "A financial vessel of faith."

### 4. Dumb & Funny (Accessible)
The best jokes need no explanation. Our humor is relatable to everyday humans who have made bad financial choices. We embrace absurdist, self-deprecating, and callback humor without the need for high-level technical jargon. We are not selling "zk-SNARK cryptography," we are selling a feeling.
