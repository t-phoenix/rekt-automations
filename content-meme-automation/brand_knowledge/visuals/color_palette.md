# Rekt CEO: Color Palette Guidelines

The Rekt CEO visual identity relies on bold, high-contrast colors that evoke urgency, rebellion, and the classic "rekt" aesthetic of the crypto trenches, juxtaposed with the luxury aspiration of being a "CEO."

## Primary Colors

The core elements of the brand structure. Heavily used in backgrounds, primary text, and framing.

*   **Rekt Red** (Primary)
    *   **Hex:** `#e7255e`
    *   **Usage:** The defining color of the brand. Represents the "red candles" of a rekt portfolio, passion, urgency, and the rebellious spirit of the movement. Used heavily for alerts, primary buttons, borders, and main accents.

*   **CEO Gold** (Secondary)
    *   **Hex:** `#F8C826`
    *   **Usage:** The aspirational color representing wealth, success, and the transition "from Rekt to Ruler." Used for secondary accents, highlight text, and premium elements (like NFT tiers or Co-Founder badges).

## Neutral Colors

Foundational colors that ensure readability and layout structure.

*   **Void Black** 
    *   **Hex:** `#0c0c0c`
    *   **Usage:** The definitive background color for all UI designs, meme bases, and dark-mode aesthetic. Represents the deep, dark trenches of crypto. Text placed on this should ALWAYS be extremely legible (e.g., pure white or high-contrast gold).

*   **Pure White**
    *   **Hex:** `#ffffff`
    *   **Usage:** Primary font color for paragraph text, UI elements against the void background, and high-contrast borders or outlines for memes.

## Border Usage in Memes
When generating memes or graphical output for the brand, the **Primary (#ff0033)** or **Secondary (#ffc400)** colors MUST be used for the distinctive Rekt CEO bounding box. 

**Standard Meme Configuration:**
- Outer background: Void Black (`#0c0c0c`) or Pure White (`#ffffff`) depending on contrast needs.
- Border stroke: Use the Rekt Red or CEO Gold to frame the "rekt" subject matter.
