# Rekt CEO: Graphics Guidelines

Our visual language is a core pillar of the Rekt CEO movement. Visual consistency builds community trust and recognizable memetic equity.

## 1. The Rekt Aesthetic ("Degen Chic")
*   **The Vibe:** Authentic, unpolished in its humor, yet intentionally framed as premium in its presentation. Think "streetwear meets the trading floor." 
*   **Imagery:** We don't use clean, corporate stock vectors. We utilize internet culture, drawn Wojaks, Peeps, stylized anime reactions, or low-res deep-fried images when conveying pure despair.
*   **The Pivot:** Images of losing are often framed against symbols of ultimate success (e.g., a McDonald's hat photoshopped onto a figure sitting in a Lamborghini) to emphasize the "From Rekt to Ruler" journey.

## 2. The Custom Boundary Rule
Every official piece of Rekt CEO generative imagery (Memes, PFPs) must feature the designated **Rekt Boundary Box** to instantly mark it as our IP.
*   **Top/Left/Right:** A thin `12px` frame.
*   **Bottom:** A thick `36px` weighted frame designed to ground the image and provide real estate for bottom text or official stamps.
*   **Corner Styling:** A strict `16px` border-radius constraint applied symmetrically to both the inner image content and the outer boundary.
*   **Colors:** Boundaries should utilize the `Primary (#e7255e)` or `Secondary (#f8c826)` brand colors based on the visual vibe of the core image.

## 3. Logo Usage
*   **Positioning:** The Rekt CEO logo should generally be used as a watermark on community assets (PFPs, generative memes) to drive organic marketing as the images virally circulate. 
*   **Placement:** Lower-right or lower-left corner, fitting comfortably within the `40px` bottom boundary frame.
*   **Clarity:** Ensure high contrast against the void/white space. Do not stretch or distort the logo.

## 4. IP and "Co-Founder" Assets
*   We emphasize that users *own* the IP of their generated content and PFPs.
*   Graphics should evoke a sense of individual identity (e.g., highly traits-customizable PFPs) while remaining instantly recognizable as part of the broader Rekt CEO collective.
