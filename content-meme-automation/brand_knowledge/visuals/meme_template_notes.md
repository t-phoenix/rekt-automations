# Rekt CEO: Meme Template Notes

Choosing the correct visual meme format is critical for conveying the correct emotional context. Below are notes on aligning community sentiment with appropriate visual architectures.

## The Categories

### 1. The "Rekt Confessional" (Despair & Acceptance)
*   **Emotion:** Self-deprecation, accepting massive financial loss, "we are all going to zero together."
*   **Visual Style:** Gloomy, Wojak variants (crying, McDonald's worker), deep-fried images, low-res, exaggerated sadness.
*   **Usage:** When highlighting the absurdity of a bad trade or referencing an industry-wide crash. 
*   **Vibe Check:** The text must be highly relatable. E.g., *"When you sell at a 90% loss just to realize there are no gas fees left to cover the transaction."*

### 2. The Anti-Establishment (Calling Out the Cabal)
*   **Emotion:** Defiance, anger channeled into humor, exposing rigged systems.
*   **Visual Style:** Contrast memes. Often showing two panels: "What retail gets" vs "What VCs get." Drake format, "Clown putting on makeup" formats, Distracted Boyfriend.
*   **Usage:** For addressing massive token unlocks, paid KOL shilling, or calling out "tech" tokens with no revenue.
*   **Vibe Check:** Punchy, rebellious. *"Me trying to hold my 0.001 ETH bag while the team unlocks another 50M tokens."*

### 3. The Copium (Delusional Optimism)
*   **Emotion:** Irony, holding 'dead bags' yet hoping for a run-up.
*   **Visual Style:** Pepe inhaling a tank of copium, "This is fine" dog, hyper-exaggerated bullish charts.
*   **Usage:** Ideal for lighthearted engagement when the market is sideways or slightly down.

### 4. From Rekt to Ruler (The Community Flex)
*   **Emotion:** Triumph, vindication, solidarity, "We made our own table."
*   **Visual Style:** High contrast, Gigachad, glowing eyes, ascendant figures, "We are the captains now" formats.
*   **Usage:** When highlighting the success of the $CEO token, breaking away from the VC system, or celebrating a community win.
*   **Vibe Check:** It shouldn't be a generic Lambo flex. It should always trace back to the fact that we got rekt first, and *then* we won together. 

---

### **AI Formatting Rule:**
When leveraging the `template_preference` engine, map the AI's determined *dominant_emotion* strictly to these templates.
*   **Despair/Sadness** -> Confessional
*   **Anger/Disgust** -> Anti-Establishment
*   **Confusion/Irony** -> Copium
*   **Joy/Triumph/Confidence** -> Ruler
