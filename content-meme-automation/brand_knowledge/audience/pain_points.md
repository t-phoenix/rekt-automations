# Pain Points: The Frustrations Rekt CEO Addresses

## 1. The Pyramid of Sophisticated Wealth Extraction
The community is deeply frustrated by the rigged mechanics of traditional crypto launches.
*   **Zero-Cost Founders & VCs:** Founders mint for free, and VCs buy massive allocations behind closed doors at steep discounts.
*   **Paid Shills:** KOLs (Key Opinion Leaders) and influencers are paid in tokens to hype projects to their followers.
*   **Retail as the Victim:** By the time the token reaches the public, retail investors are simply buying the top to provide "exit liquidity" for the insiders to dump their unlocked bags. 

## 2. The Isolation and Shame of Loss
*   **Silent Suffering:** The crypto space is obsessed with flexing "W's" (wins), lambos, and massive gains. When retail investors lose 90% of their portfolio, they feel isolated, embarrassed, and often ghost the community. 
*   **Holding "Dead" Bags:** Users are stuck with tokens that have lost all value and utility, presenting the "what to do with your coin" problem.

## 3. The Web2 Extraction Model
*   **Creating for Free:** Meme lords and shitposters create viral content that drives millions of impressions, but the Web2 platforms (Twitter, Instagram, Reddit) capture all the financial value.
*   **No IP Ownership:** Creators have their viral memes stolen or screenshotted for clout by bigger accounts, earning nothing for their cultural contribution. 

## 4. The Complexity of Web3 
*   **Minting Friction:** The traditional process of minting NFTs is painful and highly technical for average users. It involves paying exorbitant gas fees, dealing with complex smart contract approvals, listing, signing, and waiting, only to end up with an illiquid asset.
*   **Tech Over Community:** Projects prioritize complex "tech" roadmaps (that often fail to deliver) over building fun, relatable, and human experiences.
