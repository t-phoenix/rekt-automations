# Community Insights: The Language & Lore of the Trenches

## Core Conversational Themes
*   **The "Rekt Confessional":** Sharing stories of massive portfolio drawdowns, rug pulls, and terrible trades. Normalizing the emotional mess of crypto.
*   **Calling Out the Cabal:** Satirizing VCs, paid influencers, and projects with massive token unlocks that dump on retail.
*   **From Anon to Icon:** Discussions around digital identity, replacing generic avatars with unique PFPs that represent their personal Web3 journey and "emotional damage".
*   **Meme Equity:** Treating memes not just as jokes, but as cultural capital, IP, and a legitimate way to build wealth and reputation.

## Community Slang & Terminology
The community speaks a highly specific dialect of "Crypto Twitter" (CT) and degen culture. AI agents must be fluent in:
*   **Rekt:** Completely destroyed or ruined financially (e.g., a portfolio going to zero).
*   **Degen:** Short for degenerate; a trader who takes high risks, often without doing research.
*   **Ape / Aped in:** Buying into a coin recklessly or purely based on hype.
*   **Rug / Rug pull:** When developers abandon a project and run away with investors' funds.
*   **Exit Liquidity:** Retail investors who buy at the top, allowing early insiders and VCs to cash out.
*   **Diamond Hands / Paper Hands:** Refusing to sell despite massive dips (diamond) vs. panic selling early (paper).
*   **Copium / Cope:** The psychological defense mechanism used to justify holding a losing bag.
*   **WAGMI:** "We're All Gonna Make It" – a rallying cry often used ironically by the community.
*   **Alpha:** Inside information or highly valuable trading signals.
*   **Floor Price:** The lowest price of an NFT in a collection.
*   **CT:** Crypto Twitter.
*   **Bag / Bagholder:** A cryptocurrency position that has dropped significantly in value, and the person stuck holding it.
